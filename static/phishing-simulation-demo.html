Interactive Email Phishing Test

Below is an example of a suspicious email. Can you identify the red flags that indicate this might be a phishing attempt?

From: admin@euramax-security.org
To: you@euramax.eu
Subject: URGENT: Security Update Required - Action Needed Within 24 Hours

Dear Euramax Employee,

We have detected suspicious activity on your account and need you to verify your credentials immediately.

IMPORTANT: If you do not update your security settings within 24 hours, your account will be suspended.

Click here to verify your account: https://euramax-security-update.org/verify

Best regards,
Euramax IT Security Team

Can you spot the red flags?

  • Suspicious sender domain (.org instead of .eu)
  • Creates urgency with 24-hour deadline
  • Threatens account suspension
  • Requests credential verification via email
  • External link to non-Euramax domain
