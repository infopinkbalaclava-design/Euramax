Demo: Identifying Phishing Emails

Module 1 of 5

Scenario: You receive this email in your inbox

From: security@yourbankk.com
Subject: Urgent: Account Security Alert - Action Required

Dear Valued Customer,

We have detected suspicious activity on your account. For your security, we have temporarily restricted access. Please click the link below to verify your identity immediately:

Verify Account Now

Failure to verify within 24 hours will result in permanent account closure.

Thank you,
Security Team

Question: What makes this email suspicious?

Correct! ‚úì

You identified the key warning signs: the misspelled domain name "yourbankk.com" and the urgent, threatening language designed to pressure you into quick action. These are classic phishing tactics.

Key Learning Points:

  • Always check sender domains carefully for typos
  • Be suspicious of urgent threats and deadlines
  • Verify requests through official channels
  • Hover over links to see actual destinations
