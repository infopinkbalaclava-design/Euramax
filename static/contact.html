Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our phishing protection services

How quickly can we get started with Euramax?

+

Most organizations can be fully protected within 24-48 hours. Our cloud-based solution requires minimal setup, and our team will guide you through the entire onboarding process.

What email platforms do you support?

+

We support all major email platforms including Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Exchange Server, and most cloud-based email services. Our solution integrates seamlessly without disrupting your existing workflows.

How accurate is your AI detection system?

+

Our AI system maintains a 99.8% accuracy rate in detecting phishing attempts, with less than 0.01% false positives. The system continuously learns and adapts to new threats, ensuring consistent protection.

Can we customize the training content?

+

Yes! Enterprise customers can customize training content to reflect their industry, company policies, and specific threat landscape. We also offer role-based training paths for different departments and job functions.

What kind of reporting do you provide?

+

We provide comprehensive reporting including real-time threat dashboards, training progress analytics, phishing simulation results, compliance reports, and executive summaries. All reports can be customized and automated.
